Project Title:
Online Salon & Spa Booking Portal – Thai Wellness Inspired

🌅 1. Intro / Splash Screen Animation

Design a calming, premium intro experience with smooth transitions and minimalism.

Start with a warm gradient background (soft beige, cream, light gold tones)

A deep red abstract shape/section is present on screen

Animation sequence:

After 1 second, the logo softly emerges from the red section (fade + slight scale-up)

After another 1 second, the logo fades out smoothly

Then the text “Savadhika” appears from the same red section

Font style: similar to Pacifico-style script (elegant, flowing, handwritten)

After 1 second, this text fades out

Background audio suggestion: soft wind chimes (calming spa ambience)

Create a luxurious, calming Thai spa aesthetic UI with subtle animations.

🌼 Background & Elements:

Warm, neutral spa color palette (cream, gold, muted red accents)

A white lotus symbol at the top

Slight floating/hovering animation (very subtle vertical motion)

A large Buddha figure in center
